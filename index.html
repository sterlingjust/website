Hello,

My name is Sterling Just. I attended University of Wisconsin - Madison, where I graduated with a degree in Applied Mathematics, Engineering, and Physics (AMEP). While enrolled, I started taking courses in what had up until then only been a hobby: photography. I loved having projects and being pushed to shoot with purpose. I developed this website as a portfolio for my photography projects, as well (eventually) other projects that I'm working on.

This website was developed using HTML, CSS, and Javascript in Adobe Dreamweaver.
